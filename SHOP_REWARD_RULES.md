# Smart Mudra Shop Reward Rules

This document outlines the cashback discount rules configured for shops on the Smart Mudra platform. The platform applies custom rules for specific shops, and falls back to a default set of rules for any shop that does not have custom rules defined.

## Custom Shop Rules

### 1. Srujan Kids House
This shop uses a probability-based distribution for its rewards, rather than fixed tiered bill ranges. 

**Minimum Bill Required:** ₹100
* **90% Chance:** 5% - 10% Cashback
* **7% Chance:** 7% - 8% Cashback
* **3% Chance:** 10% Cashback

### 2. Sandesh Agro Machinery
This shop uses tiered percentages based on the total purchase amount.

* **₹0 - ₹2,000:** 5% - 7% Cashback
* **₹2,000 - ₹10,000:** 5% Cashback
* **₹10,000 - ₹50,000:** 4% Cashback
* **Above ₹50,000:** 2% - 3% Cashback

### 3. Rahul Agency
This shop uses tiered percentages based on the total purchase amount.

* **₹0 - ₹1,000:** 10% - 15% Cashback
* **₹1,000 - ₹5,000:** 10% - 20% Cashback
* **₹5,000 - ₹10,000:** 10% - 15% Cashback
* **₹10,000 - ₹50,000:** 2% - 7% Cashback

---

## Default Shop Rules

For all other active shops without explicit customizations, the following default discount tiers apply based on the bill total:

* **₹50 - ₹500:** 8% - 15% Cashback
* **₹500 - ₹1,000:** 5% - 10% Cashback
* **₹1,000 - ₹2,000:** 7% - 15% Cashback
* **₹2,000 - ₹3,500:** 5% - 10% Cashback
* **₹3,500 - ₹6,000:** 5% - 8% Cashback
* **₹6,000 - ₹10,000:** 4% - 6% Cashback

> Note: All calculated reward points are subject to additional platform caps (e.g., maximum global limit of 1000 points, or individual shop maximums) and are ultimately rounded to the nearest multiple of 10 before being granted to the customer.
