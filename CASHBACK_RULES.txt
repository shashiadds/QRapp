Smart Mudra Cashback / Points Rules
===================================

Note
----
The app now shows rewards as "points", not rupees or cashback. This document lists the current cashback-style point rules used by the reward engine.


Global rules
------------
1. Minimum purchase total required to submit: 10.
2. Minimum points before final purchase cap: 10.
3. Global maximum points before final purchase cap: 1000.
4. Shop maximum points also applies through each shop's maxReward value.
5. Final awarded points can never exceed the purchase total.
   Example: purchase total 11 can receive at most 11 points.
6. Percentage-based points are rounded down to the nearest 10 before caps are applied.
7. Percentage slab rules are checked first.
8. Fixed/probability reward bands are used only when no percentage slab matches the purchase total.


Default percentage slabs
------------------------
These apply to most shops unless a special shop-specific percentage rule exists.

Purchase total 50 to 500:
- Random percentage: 8% to 15%

Purchase total 500 to 1000:
- Random percentage: 5% to 10%

Purchase total 1000 to 2000:
- Random percentage: 7% to 15%

Purchase total 2000 to 3500:
- Random percentage: 5% to 10%

Purchase total 3500 to 6000:
- Random percentage: 5% to 8%

Purchase total 6000 to 10000:
- Random percentage: 4% to 6%

Operational note
----------------
If shops are loaded from Google Sheets, their shop names, status, maxReward, costPerScan, and rewardBands may differ from the seed shop rules above. The global rules and percentage-slab precedence still apply in the current engine.
